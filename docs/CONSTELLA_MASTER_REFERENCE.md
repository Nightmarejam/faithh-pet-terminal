### The Resonance Gap
‚úÖ **KNOWN**: The misalignment between a person's will (what they need/want) and their world's frequency (what their environment provides). This gap creates friction, loss of meaning, and systemic harm when institutionalized.

**Key insight**: The gap isn't personal failure‚Äîit's a signal that systems need adjustment.

### Harmonic Alignment
‚úÖ **KNOWN**: Human flourishing requires coherence across three domains:
- **Body** - Physical health and environment
- **Mind** - Mental clarity and purpose
- **World** - Social and institutional context

### The Role of Dissonance
‚úÖ **KNOWN**: Dissonance is **not an enemy to erase**. It's:
- A natural signal indicating misalignment
- Often creative and generative
- Harmful only when chronic and unresolved

### The Cycle of Restoration
‚úÖ **KNOWN**:
1. **Recognize** the dissonance
2. **Adjust** the environment (not just the person)
3. **Build** micro-resonances (small wins)
4. **Embody** the new alignment
5. **Scale** to community level

### The Human Standard
‚úÖ **KNOWN**: "Judge systems by how they treat those with the weakest resonance."

### The Oath of Resonance
‚úÖ **KNOWN**: "Design structures that reduce the resonance gap before judging performance."
