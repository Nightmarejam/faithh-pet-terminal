## üìê Generation Settings & Tips

### Recommended Leonardo AI Settings:
- **Model for Characters:** Leonardo Phoenix or DreamShaper v7
- **Model for UI Mockups:** Leonardo Kino XL or SDXL
- **Resolution:** 1024x1024 (avatars), 1920x1080 (UI mockups)
- **Guidance Scale:** 7-10 for balanced results
- **Number of Images:** 4 variations per prompt
- **Negative Prompts:** "blurry, low quality, distorted, asymmetric, messy, cluttered"

### Color Codes for Consistency:
